About Rebbe Points

Rebbe Points is a classroom management system designed to help teachers and administrators track student progress, manage rewards, and maintain engagement through a points-based system.

Features include:

  • Student points tracking
  • Reward system management
  • Class and student history
  • Administrator controls for school-wide management

How to Use Rebbe Points

Follow these steps to get started with Rebbe Points:

  1. Create an account or sign in as a teacher or administrator.
  2. Set up your classes and add students.
  3. Create rewards to motivate your students.
  4. Award points for positive behaviors and track progress.
  5. Use the leaderboard and history to monitor student engagement.
